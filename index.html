About Modern Mans Guide to Dating

MMG is a non-profit, community based group. MMG provides coaching and tools to facilitate dating and creating amazing relationships.

The coaching and tools address how to:

  • Approach in any situation
  • Carrying conversations
  • Creating attraction
  • Having great dates
  • Dressing and creating an image that fits you
  • Creating amazing relationships
  • Creating and building a social circle
